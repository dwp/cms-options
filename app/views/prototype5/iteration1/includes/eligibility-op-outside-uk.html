You cannot use the Child Maintenance Service

You cannot use the Child Maintenance Service because the other parent lives outside of the UK and does not work for a British organisation.

Find out how to get a child maintenance decision if the other parent lives abroad
You may be able to get a child maintenance decision recognised or enforced if the other parent live in another country.

Find out how to arrange child maintenance yourself
You can make your own maintenance arrangements if one or both parents live abroad. No one else has to be involved. You and the other parent can organise everything yourselves.
