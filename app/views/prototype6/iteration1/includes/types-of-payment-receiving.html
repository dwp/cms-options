Receiving child maintenance

If you choose to use the Child Maintenance Service there is a £20 application fee. You will not have to pay this fee if you are under 19 years old or have experienced domestic abuse.

There are 2 ways you can get payments.

Direct Pay

The other parent will pay child maintenance directly to your bank account.

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent and there are no additional fees.

Collect and Pay

The Child Maintenance Service will collect maintenance from the other parent and pass it to you.

You can only start with Collect and Pay if the other parent:

  • refuses to respond to us
  • has missed previous payments (and there is evidence of this)
  • agrees to use Collect and Pay

You can change to Collect and Pay later if Direct Pay does not work.

There are extra fees for this service, which are:

  • 20% will be added to the other parent's payment
  • 4% will be taken off the payment you receive

For example, if the payment amount was £100, you would get £96 and the other parent would pay £120.
