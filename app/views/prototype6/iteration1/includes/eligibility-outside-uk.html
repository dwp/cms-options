You cannot use the Child Maintenance Service

You cannot use the Child Maintenance Service because you live outside of the UK and do not work for a British organisation.

Find out how to arrange child maintenance yourself
You can make your own maintenance arrangements if one or both parents live abroad. No one else has to be involved. You and the other parent can organise everything yourselves.

Find out how to get a child maintenance decision if you live abroad
You may be able to get a child maintenance decision recognised or enforced if you live in another country.
