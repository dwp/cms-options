Your options

Make your own child maintenance arrangement

You can make your own maintenance arrangements between parents. No one else has to be involved and it can be whatever you agree. For example, you and the other parent can:

  • decide how much you will pay
  • change the amount when needed
  • decide how often you will make payments
  • agree for you to pay for specific things, like school uniforms or trips

Making your own arrangement might be a good option if:

  • you feel safe dealing with the other parent
  • you think the other parent will stick to an agreement
