Work out the cost of raising your children

This tool will help you work out expenses for your children if you're arranging child maintenance yourselves.

Use this tool to:

  • work out how much you and the other parent or guardian spend on your children.
  • decide what items in your child maintenance agreement.
  • agree who is going to pay for what and how much child maintenance one parent should pay the other.
Start now

Before you start

You'll need to know how much you and the other parent spend on essential and larger items for your children. You may want to have your bank statement and receipts available to help enter your costs.
