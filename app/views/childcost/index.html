Child cost calculator

Use this tool as a guide to help you think about the items you would like to include in your child maintenance arrangement.

You might find this tool is a useful way of sorting out who’s going to pay for what and how much the maintenance contribution should be.

The items each child may need depends on their age – you may find things listed that you do not want to include, and there may be things that you want to add.

There is also a child maintenance calculator that you can use to work out an amount of child maintenance for your children.
