Paying and receiving child maintenance

If you choose to use the Child Maintenance Service there is a £20 application fee. You will not have to pay this fee if you are under 19 years old or have experienced domestic abuse.

There are 2 ways payments can be made.

Direct Pay

The paying parent will pay maintenance directly to the receiving parent’s bank account.

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent and there are no additional fees.

Collect and Pay

The Child Maintenance Service will collect maintenance from the paying parent and pass it to the receiving parent.

You can only start with Collect and Pay if the paying parent:

  • refuses to respond to us
  • has missed previous payments (and there is evidence of this)
  • agrees to use Collect and Pay

Collect and Pay can be enforced if the paying parent stops responding to us or stops making payments.

There are extra fees for this service, which are:

  • 20% (added to the payment) for paying parents
  • 4% (taken off the payment) for receiving parents

For example, if the payment amount was £100, the paying parent would pay £120 and the receiving parent would get £96.
