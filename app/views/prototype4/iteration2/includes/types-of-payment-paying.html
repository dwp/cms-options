Paying child maintenance

If you choose to use the Child Maintenance Service there is a £20 application fee. You will not have to pay this fee if you are under 19 years old or have experienced domestic abuse.

There are 2 ways you can make payments.

Direct Pay

You’ll pay maintenance directly into the other parent’s bank account.

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent and there are no additional fees.

Collect and Pay

The Child Maintenance Service will collect maintenance from you and pass it to the other parent.

You can only start with Collect and Pay if the other parent agrees to it.

Collect and Pay can be enforced if you stop responding to us or stop making payments.

There are extra fees for this service, which are:

  • 20% (added to your payment)
  • 4% (taken off the payment the other parent receives)

For example, if the payment amount was £100, you would pay £120 and the other parent would get £96.
