Use the Child Maintenance Service

If you are worried about contacting the other parent:

  • you can speak to your bank or builidng society about setting up an account that will not give away your location
  • you do not need to have any contact with the other parent - the Child Maintenance Service can contact them for you

We will need to share the following details with the other parent:

  • your name
  • your child's name and birthday
  • the number of days your child stays with the parent who will be making payments - the other parent will need to confirm this
  • we will not share anything else with the other parent unless you ask us
